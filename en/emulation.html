What is it ?

I'm going to talk here about virtual game emulation. Portability is one of the reasons for emulating a game. But it's also a way of preserving a launch medium. Developers code a game for a specific machine or console, if we don't take into account the portability of certain games. For the average user, to play a video game, it's essential to own its launch medium. Emulation offers the possibility of moving this support to the machine or console you're using.
